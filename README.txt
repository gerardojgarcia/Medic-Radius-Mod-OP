
============================================================
                     MEDIC RANGE – OP
============================================================

This mod dramatically boosts the autoheal and revive-zone
radius of all medics in Call to Arms: Gates of Hell – Ostfront.
It is intentionally overpowered and meant for sandbox play,
large-scale battles, cinematic missions, or simply for fun.

------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT THIS MOD CHANGES
------------------------------------------------------------

1. medic_zone2.def  
   - The healing aura around the medic has been increased from
     the default 15 meters to an extreme 50 meters.

2. medic_zone.def  
   - The placeable revive/heal zone has been expanded from
     the default 15 meters to 35 meters.

Medics will now heal almost everything in a massive area,
requiring little to no micromanagement. This can greatly alter
game balance, but is excellent for creative or casual scenarios.

------------------------------------------------------------
INSTALLATION
------------------------------------------------------------

Place this folder inside:

resource/mods/
or
<game_root>/mods/

Enable "Medic Range – OP" in the in-game Mods menu.

Restart the game after enabling the mod.

------------------------------------------------------------
MULTIPLAYER NOTE
------------------------------------------------------------

All players must have this mod enabled, or desync may occur.
Not recommended for competitive multiplayer due to extreme buffs.

------------------------------------------------------------
COMPATIBILITY
------------------------------------------------------------

- Compatible with game versions 1.058 – 1.060
- Will conflict with any other mod that changes medic_zone.def
  or medic_zone2.def
- Safe to load in existing saves
